Eco-Friendly products being used all over Sri Lanka are only made possible by the work of many companies and brands in Sri Lanka, some more well known compared to others, but all having the same goal in mind: to reduce waste and keep Sri Lanka Eco-Friendly to save our environment.

A few of the companies which make this possible

Back to Earth

Formed in 1994, this company specializes in creating stationary products such as pencils, notebooks and paper dispensers made from recycled paper and throwaway wood primarily from plantations.

Visit their website here

 

Nook and Fork

Nook and Fork is a company that has their own line of biodegradable products in the form of cutlery and bags. Their products are aesthetically pleasing in the form of how they look and their usability.

Visit their website here

Eco Kadé

A small family-owned business, their aim is to reduce the use of single plastic and other non-renewable products and make organic and natural soaps as well as other products like bamboo cups.

Visit their website here
