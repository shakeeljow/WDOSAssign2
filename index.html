What it means and how we can achieve it

What does Eco-Friendly mean?

Eco-Friendly, or Environment Friendly, is anything that can be used or done which would not affect the environment or ecosystems negatively.

This can be achieved using many different methods, such as using biodegradable products or even reducing the amount of energy and electricity used in your own house.

Transport

Changing your mode of transport to something more environmetally friendly, which include:

  • Electric cars
  • Bicycles
  • Public transport

would result in less pollution.

Energy

The creation of electricity has by far the worst effect on pollution due to the burning of fossil fuels which release harmful gases into the atmosphere.

Sources of renewable energy, such as:

  1. Wind Energy
  2. Solar Energy
  3. Hydro-electricity

would allow for a sustainable way of creating energy and electricity, thus making them suitable for the environment.
